Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

Lanternfall (the “Game”, “we”, “us”) is a mobile game developed by Kyrzma. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you play Lanternfall and how it is used. By playing the Game, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Lanternfall is an offline, single-player game. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not ourselves collect personal information such as your name, email, or contacts.

The Game shows advertisements through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob and its partners may collect and process:

  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID)
  • Device information (model, operating-system version, language)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (country/region level)
  • Ad interaction data (ads shown, clicks)

This data is not used by us to personally identify you.

2. How Information Is Used

Data collected by AdMob is used to display ads (including personalized ads where permitted), to measure ad performance, and to prevent fraud and abuse. Ads are provided by Google. For details, see Google’s Privacy Policy and how Google uses data from partner apps.

3. In-App Purchases

The Game may offer in-app purchases (for example, removing ads). Purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment details; payment is handled entirely by Google.

4. Children’s Privacy

Lanternfall is not directed to children under 13 and is not designed as a children’s app. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided data, please contact us and we will remove it.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • Reset or limit ad tracking: Android → Settings → Google → Ads → “Reset advertising ID” or “Opt out of Ads Personalization”.
  • Consent (EU/UK/EEA): Where required, a consent prompt is shown before personalized ads; you can change your choice at any time.
  • GDPR / CCPA: You may request access to, or deletion of, data associated with your device by contacting us. Note that ad data is held by Google, not us.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Advertising data is processed and shared with Google AdMob and its advertising partners solely to deliver and measure ads.

7. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Any ad-related data is retained by Google in accordance with Google’s policies.

8. Security

Data transmitted for advertising is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). No system is perfectly secure, but we rely on Google’s infrastructure and industry-standard protections.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
